Platform Overview

Magento offers unmatched flexibility and scalability, but that power comes with complexity. Large catalogues, intricate integrations and peak sales periods can overwhelm standard hosting.

Corefinity’s platform is purpose built for Magento, delivering the performance, security and stability needed to keep stores fast and revenue flowing, no matter the traffic or customisation.

With Corefinity, you get:
  • Consistent checkout performance during promotions and peaks.
  • A support team that have been working with Magento since the early days.
  • Safe releases with staging, rollbacks and zero-downtime deploys for updates.
