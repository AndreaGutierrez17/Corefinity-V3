Corefinity was founded by Magento developers who were frustrated with slow, inflexible hosting and set out to build the fastest, most reliable eCommerce stack. Our platform is engineered by certified developers and engineers with years of hands-on experience supporting Magento, Shopware and other leading platforms. Read more about our story
